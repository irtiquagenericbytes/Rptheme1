Elevate Efficiency with Dedicated Team Out-staffing

The out-staffing model offers clients the flexibility to extend their team with dedicated resources from our highly skilled professionals. This approach allows for seamless integration with the client's existing team and processes while providing direct management and control over the outsourced staff. It is a cost-effective solution for project execution with monthly service packages available.

STEP 1

Requirements

Requirements are the project's fundamental roadmap, encompassing its objectives, functionalities, and scope. They play a critical role in project planning and execution, ensuring alignment with stakeholder expectations and successful project outcomes.

STEP 2

Team Selection / Cost Estimation

Team Selection is about assembling the right talent mix for project success. Cost Estimation is the strategic process of forecasting expenses to ensure efficient budget management. Together, they form the foundation for effective project planning and execution.

STEP 3

Sprints /Updates Planning

Sprints are iterative work cycles within agile project management, allowing teams to focus on specific tasks and goals within a defined timeframe. Updates Planning involves strategizing and scheduling when and how new features or improvements will be incorporated into the project. Together, these practices ensure ongoing progress and alignment with project objectives.

STEP 4

Team Allocation

Team Allocation is the strategic assignment of skilled individuals to specific roles and tasks within a project. This process ensures that the right talent is utilized efficiently, enhancing teamwork and productivity, and ultimately contributing to the project's successful execution.

STEP 5

Task Execution

Task Execution is the practical implementation of project activities and assignments. It involves carrying out planned tasks, meeting deadlines, and achieving project milestones. Effective task execution is essential for project success, ensuring that the project stays on track and objectives are met.

STEP 6

Client Review & Feedback

Client Review & Feedback is a crucial phase in project development. It involves presenting project progress and results to clients, allowing them to provide valuable insights and opinions. This iterative process ensures client satisfaction and aligns the project with their expectations, fostering a collaborative and successful partnership.
